    What is it

    The Imaginatio is an academic space for the Multimedia Engineering program at the Universidad Militar Nueva Granada, where students have the opportunity to connect with professionals, graduates, and companies in the fields of engineering and multimedia design.
    The event seeks to reflect on the different perspectives around the concept of multimedia, from the perspective of students, graduates, academics, and industry leaders.
